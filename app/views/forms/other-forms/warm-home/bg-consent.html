Warm Home Discount

The government and energy suppliers are running a scheme to help those at risk from fuel poverty in England, Scotland and Wales get a rebate from their electricity bill. If you are eligible, the Warm Home Discount will offer you a benefit of Â£140 (inclusive of VAT) against your electricity bill in 2020/21.
