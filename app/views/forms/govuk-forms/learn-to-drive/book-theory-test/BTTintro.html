About this form

Go to the forms menu

It looks like the real form but it's a bit different and simplified

Please note:

  • the first page is the GOVUK website, the rest of the form is on the DVSA which looks a bit different
  • you can only do the car booking (booking for a motorcyle is very similar so not done here)
  • you cannot chose a centre: whatever postcode you enter, it will only show you 2 centres and always assumed you picked the Glasgow one
  • you cannot chose a date or chose a time at the moment, it is chosen for you
  • the support options have been simplified
  • you cannot proceed to the payment page - we didn't have access to these pages so could not reproduce
  • there is no error message: if you forget to add some required information for example, you will be able to go to the next step anyway

At any time, selecting the logo at the top left will take you back to the start (forms menu)
