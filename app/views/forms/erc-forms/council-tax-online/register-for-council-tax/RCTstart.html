Council Tax Online

Important Information

Self Service Plus users - update your profile to Council Tax Online and keep your login details by selecting to Sign In

The Council Tax Online service enables residents of East Renfrewshire to manage their own personal Council Tax details online. You can check your bill, request a copy bill, see what payments you have made and make further payments. To sign up for Council Tax Online, you need to

  • Have an email address
  • Confirm your identity to us
  • Have a Council Tax bill for your current property

We take the security of your data seriously and need to have proof of your identity before you can access personal information. You'll be required to produce 2 forms of identification

  • One photographic, such as a passport and
  • One other, such as a current utility bill

Once you have proved your identity, you will be able to use your Council Tax bill to get access to your billing and payment details.

All fields marked * are mandatory and must be completed.
