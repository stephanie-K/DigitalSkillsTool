Test centre information

Erskine House is directly across the Clyde Shopping Centre on Kilbowe Road. It is situated on the right hand side entry into the Clydebank Business Park off Kilbowie Road. Singer railway station is approx 200 metres from the premises.

Full disabled access is available. Wheelchair access is via a ramp and lifts are available for access to the first floor.
