Warm Home Discount

The government and energy suppliers are running a scheme to help those at risk from fuel poverty in England, Scotland and Wales get a rebate from their electricity bill. If you are eligible, the Warm Home Discount will offer you a benefit of £140 (inclusive of VAT) against your electricity bill in 2020/21.

When will I get it?

We’re working hard to credit all Warm Home Discounts between January and March 2021 If you’ve got a prepay meter, we’ll send you a voucher to take to the Post Office. And for everyone else, you’ll see a Warm Home Discount credit on your bill or statement.

Do I need to send any documents?

For security, our system randomly picks some customers to provide evidence of their benefits and income. Don’t worry if that includes you.

You’ll get an email or letter with full details of what we need and how to provide it. Just be sure to keep an eye on your inbox and spam folder if you’ve given us an email address.

Can I change which fuel gets the discount?

Yes. But please wait until after you’ve received the credit, then let us know if you want to transfer it to your other fuel. You can do that by starting a Live Chat from our website.

What if I change supplier?

The Warm Home Discount Scheme is run by your electricity supplier (even if you ask for the credit on your gas account). So if you change electricity supplier before you get the credit from us, you'll need to reapply to your new provider.
