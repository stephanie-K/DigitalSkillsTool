How do I read my meter?
Meters come in all shapes and sizes. You can find out how to read yours in our extensive meter reading guide.
How are my readings used to calculate my bill?

Meter readings you submit three days before your payment are used directly to calculate your bill.

If you submit a meter reading at another time, we use it to adjust your estimated usage. The more meter readings you submit, the more accurate this estimate can be. Submitting meter readings every month ensures this estimate will be very close to your actual usage.
