Children who live with you

To apply for Universal Credit for your children you must be the main carer. If you share childcare with an ex-partner, you must agree between you who is the main carer.

You can only apply for children who are:

  • living with you
  • under 20 years old

Include:

  • children, step-children and adopted children you are responsible for
  • children whose parents do not live with you

Do not include:

  • foster children
  • children who are away, and who will be away for 6 months or more
  • children whose parents or carers also live with you
