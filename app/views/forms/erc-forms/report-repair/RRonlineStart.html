Repairs

Report a non-emergency repair

This process is for you to report a repair for your Council house. We aim to contact you within 5 working days to discuss your request. Further details on our service are available on our Housing Repairs page.

Emergencies

Please do not use this service for any emergency repair, for example, a burst water pipe that may cause flooding; instead contact us by telephone.

  • During Office Hours 0141 577 3700 (Monday to Friday 8.45-4.45pm)
  • Out of Hours 08080 031601

    • For issues relating to Gas repairs or servicing, please contact our contractor SALTIRE on 0800 0428 2710 (free 24-hour service)
