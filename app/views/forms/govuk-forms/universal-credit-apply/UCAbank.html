What counts as a bank account?

You can use a:

  • bank account
  • building society account
  • credit union account

    • You may also have to confirm the details at the jobcentre.

    It must be a UK account.

    I don't have any kind of bank account

    A bank or building society account will make it easier for you to get Universal Credit.

    If you cannot get one right away, you may be able to use a Post Office card account instead. You will not be able to stay on a card account for long. We will ask you to move to a standard bank account after you have made your claim.

    If you cannot get any sort of account, call 0800 328 5644 (Textphone: 0800 328 1344). You can call Monday or Friday, 8am to 6pm (closed bank holidays).
