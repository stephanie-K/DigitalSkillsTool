If you have a medical condition

You will normally need to complete a medical questionnaire. If this is required our medical team will contact you shortly after you have finished this application. The questionnaire also enables you to provide your consent for our Medical Adviser to request medical information from your doctor(s) if this is needed.

If at all possible, we will make a decision based on the information you provide on the questionnaire. However, if further information is required, then our Medical Adviser may:

  • Contact your own doctor and/or consultant
  • Arrange for you to be examined by a locally appointed medical officer or local consultant/specialist
  • Ask you to undergo a driving assessment, eyesight or driving test
