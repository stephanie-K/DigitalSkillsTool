Test centre information

Washington Street in situated off the Broomielaw before the Kingston Bridge underpass on the right hand side if travelling from City Centre direction.

Pedestrian access is also situated if travelling from the City Centre along Argyle Street before reaching the Clydeside Expressway and across from the Marriot Hotel. A set of pedestrian stairs will lead you down to Washington Street and the building is located on the right hand side after approx 100 yards.

Anderson rail station is a five minute walk away and Glasgow Central is just 10 minutes away. Local bus services provide direct access to the arterial bus routes to north and south Glasgow.

Full Disabled Access: Lift solely for disabled candidates at entrance to building thereafter lift to the third floor where testing takes place in room 325.
