Would you like to get text messages from us?

This is so we can let you know about the progress of your application, our feedback survey, or any other Social Security Scotland benefits you might be able to get.

Can we use your email address?

We need to send your email address to allpay to set up your online Best Start Foods account. We'll also use this to let you know about the progress of your application, our feedback survey, or any other Social Security Scotland benefits you might be able to get.



Social Security Scotland will only keep hold of the latest email address you have provided. This will mean any previous email addresses supplied will be replaced. This may affect the communications you get and access to your allpay account.
