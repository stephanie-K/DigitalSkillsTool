What will happen next?

DVLA supports the NHS organ donor registration scheme.

DVLA is pleased to facilitate the scheme, passing your name, address, date of birth and gender to NHS Blood and Transplant (NHSBT) for the purpose of recording your wishes to become an organ donor on the NHS Organ Donor Register.

NHSBT will match your details with your NHS number, add your name to the NHS Organ Donor Register and write to you to confirm this.

It occasionally happens that people have forgotten registering on a previous occasion. If NHSBT already hold your details they will update your current entry from the information given today, but to keep down administrative costs, will not write to confirm this to you.

For further information, please visit www.organdonation.nhs.uk or call 0300 123 23 23.

Thank you again for registering and please do discuss organ donation with family and friends so they are aware of your wishes.
