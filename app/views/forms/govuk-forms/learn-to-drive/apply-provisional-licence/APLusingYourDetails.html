Using your personal details

What we do about security

Secure connections and encryption

DVLA needs these personal details to complete this transaction. Your personal details are used to:

  • check your identity
  • check if a driver record exists for you
  • create your photocard licence when all validation checks have been completed

Your personal details include:

  • title - select one of the options on the list - if the option you need does not appear on the list, select 'Other' and enter the title you need
  • surname - enter your last name here
  • forename(s) - enter your first name here - if you have middle name(s), enter them here leaving a space between each name
  • gender - select one of the options on the list
  • date of birth - select the day, month and year in which you were born
  • country of birth - select the country where you were born
